about ACA Foundation

Adunoye Charity Aid Foundation is a private independent philanthropic organisation committed to improving the quality of life of Nigerians, by supporting the youths and alleviating the suffering of the less privileged in our society. The Foundation is committed to providing support, in human capacity development, health, academics and skills acquisition. Adunoye foundation was established on 22nd of June 2020. By a young and useful Youth philanthropist from Ekiti LGA of Kwara State in Nigeria. PRINCE JESUFEMI OYEYEMI ADEYEMI. Our Mission is to transform the lives of many people in the society, to answer the basic demand of their livelihood, and our vision is to ensure welfare and provide aid to the needy and poor people, to eradicate poor/rich inferiority complex in the society.
