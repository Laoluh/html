about ACA Foundation

Adunoye Charity Aid Foundation is a private independent philanthropic organisation committed to improving the quality of life of Nigerians, by supporting/alleviating the suffering of the less privileged in our society and is committed to providing support in the areas of human capacity development, health, academics and skills acquisition. Adunoye foundation was established in 22nd of June 2020 by a young and useful Youth philanthropist from Ekiti LGA of Kwara State in Nigeria by PRINCE JESUFEMI OYEYEMI ADEYEMI.

Mission Statement

To transform the lives of many people in the society,to aid them in meeting their basic demand of livelihood.

Vision Statement
